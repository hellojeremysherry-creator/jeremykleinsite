Business & Professions Code §§ 6060–6061

These sections of the California Business and Professions Code set out the statutory requirements for admission to the practice of law, including the LOSP pathway under § 6060(e)(2)(B) and the first-year law students’ examination requirement and exemption under § 6060(h).
